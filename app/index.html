About Social Studios

SocialStudios brings the unparalleled visual impact of television to web content.

Videos are the most engaging rich media format, but because video production is expensive and requires talent, publishers face obstacles to producing compelling video content at scale.

SocialStudios' proprietary software platform, which combines algorithmic strength and crowdsourcing functionality, allows publishers to transform original web content into engaging, promotional videos viewable on all devices. These videos enrich their source web pages and extend the reach and brand of publishers via promotional placement on social and video networks.
